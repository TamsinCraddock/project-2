Welcome to Quiz Me!

Put your general knowledge to the ultimate test.

The rules of play are simple:

  1. Read each question carefully.
  2. Select the letter corresponding to your chosen answer. You will not be able to move on to the next question until you have chosen an answer for the current question.
  3. You will immediately know if your answeris 'Correct' or 'Incorrect'.
  4. Click next to move on to the next question.
  5. You will receive a total score at the end of the game.
  6. Good Luck!

A brand new set of questions will be added daily!
